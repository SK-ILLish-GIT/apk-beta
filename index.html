About

An apex nucleating institute and globally recognized leader in the area of Information Technology, Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahabad, established in 1999, strengthens the ingenious capability necessary for harnessing multidimensional facets of IT at all levels. Aparoksha, the annual technical event of IIIT Allahabad has a reach extended far and wide across the country and beyond, and has managed to become one of the largest and most awaited events for technocrats and young innovators to showcase their ingenuity. Be it raging robotics to endless programming and inspirational talks by esteemed keynote speakers to stimulating workshops and exuberant pro-nights filled with laughter and entertainment, Aparoksha has witnessed the pinnacle of awe-striking technological events over the past sixteen editions as every edition strives to take a leap forward in redefining and revolutionizing technology.
